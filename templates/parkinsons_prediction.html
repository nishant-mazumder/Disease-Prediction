About Parkinson's Disease Prediction

Our Parkinson's disease prediction model analyzes voice characteristics and other indicators to assess your risk of developing Parkinson's disease. The model uses advanced signal processing techniques to detect subtle changes in voice patterns.

Early Signs:
  • Voice changes (soft, hoarse, or monotone)
  • Tremor in hands, arms, legs, or jaw
  • Stiffness or slowness of movement
  • Balance problems
  • Loss of smell
Management Tips:
  • Regular exercise
  • Physical therapy
  • Speech therapy
  • Medication adherence
  • Support groups
  • Regular medical follow-up
